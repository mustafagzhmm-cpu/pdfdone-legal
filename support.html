Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the subscription/paywall screen in the app and tap Restore. Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID used for the purchase.

Are my documents uploaded to your servers?

PDFDone processes documents on-device for core features. Your files are not uploaded to our servers for normal signing, scanning, or editing workflows.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. You can cancel anytime from Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

I found a bug in signing or scan flow. What should I send?

Please send a short description, steps to reproduce, and a screenshot or screen recording if possible. If the issue is PDF-specific, mention whether it happens in Sign PDF, Scan & Sign, or Batch Sign.
