Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how PDFDone (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use the PDFDone mobile application (the “App”).

Last updated:

Short version: Your documents are processed on-device. We do not sell your data.

1) Information We Collect

  • Documents and files you choose: PDFs, photos, scans, signatures, and edits you create in the App.
  • Purchase information: Subscription status provided by Apple via StoreKit.
  • Support communications: Information you send us when contacting support.
  • Usage limits: We may store limited usage-related information locally on your device to enforce free usage limits. This data is not transmitted off the device.

2) How We Use Information

  • To provide PDF editing, signing, and export features.
  • To manage subscriptions and Pro access.
  • To respond to user support requests.

3) On-Device Processing

PDFDone processes documents primarily on your device. We do not store your documents on external servers.

4) Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data. Files are shared only when you explicitly export or share them.

5) Third-Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit: Handles purchases and subscriptions according to Apple’s policies.

The App currently does not use third-party analytics or crash reporting tools.

6) Permissions

  • Camera – for scanning documents (optional).
  • Photos – for importing images (optional).
  • Files – for selecting PDFs (optional).

7) Data Retention

Temporary files may be stored locally on your device during editing. You control your data and can remove it at any time.

8) Children’s Privacy

PDFDone is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect children’s data.

9) Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.

10) Contact

If you have questions, contact us at:

Email: pdfdone@outlook.com

This policy is provided for informational purposes and is intended to comply with Apple App Store Review Guidelines.
