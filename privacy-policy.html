Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PDFDone (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use the PDFDone mobile application (the “App”).

Short version: Your documents are processed on-device. We do not sell or track you.

1. Information We Collect

  • Documents and files: PDFs, photos, scans, signatures, and edits you create or import in the App.
  • Purchase information: Subscription status provided by Apple via StoreKit.
  • Support communications: Information you send when contacting support.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide PDF editing, signing, scanning, and export features.
  • To manage subscriptions and Pro feature access.
  • To respond to user support requests.

3. On-Device Processing

PDFDone processes documents primarily on your device. We do not upload or store your documents on external servers.

4. Free Usage Limits

Free users can perform a limited number of final actions per day (such as export, save, or share).

To enforce daily limits and prevent abuse, the App stores a local usage counter securely on the device using the iOS Keychain. This data never leaves your device and is not linked to your identity.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data. Files are shared only when you explicitly choose to export or share them.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit: Handles purchases and subscriptions according to Apple’s policies.

The App does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

7. Permissions

  • Camera: For scanning documents (optional).
  • Photos: For importing images (optional).
  • Files: For selecting PDFs (optional).

8. Data Retention

Temporary files may be stored locally on your device during editing. You control your data and can remove it at any time.

9. Children’s Privacy

PDFDone is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect children’s personal data.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
